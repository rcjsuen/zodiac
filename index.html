Zodiac

Zodiac is an application backed by HTML5 and JavaScript for studying English.

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

About

Zodiac was born when I realized just how hard it was for some of my students to spell out English words. Flashcards are portable but they cannot be easily shared. Zodiac provides a way for people to easily create and share flashcards due to the simple format of the input files.
